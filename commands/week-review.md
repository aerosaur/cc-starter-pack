---
description: Create a weekly summary from recent work
---

Generate a comprehensive weekly review by analyzing recent work. Follow these steps:

## 1. Gather Information

### From Memory Keeper (if available)
- Get items from the past 7 days
- Look for all categories: `progress`, `decision`, `note`, `task`
- Group by project or topic

### From Conversation History
- Review conversations from this week
- Extract key accomplishments and decisions

## 2. Synthesize Weekly Patterns

Analyze the week to identify:
- **Major Themes**: What were the main focus areas?
- **Completed Milestones**: What significant work was finished?
- **Key Decisions**: Important technical or business decisions
- **Challenges**: Issues encountered and how they were addressed
- **Learnings**: New skills, insights, or knowledge gained

## 3. Create Weekly Summary

```markdown
# Weekly Review - [Week of Month DD-DD, YYYY]

## Overview
[2-3 sentences capturing the essence of the week]

## Key Accomplishments
1. [Major achievement 1]
2. [Major achievement 2]
3. [Major achievement 3]

## Technical Work Completed
- [Feature/fix 1]
- [Feature/fix 2]

## Important Decisions
- [Decision 1 with context]
- [Decision 2 with context]

## Challenges & Solutions
- [Challenge 1]: [How it was addressed]

## Learnings
- [New insight or skill]

## Next Week's Focus
1. [Priority 1]
2. [Priority 2]
3. [Priority 3]
```

## 4. Display and Save

1. Display the full weekly review
2. Ask if user wants to save it (to file or memory-keeper)

## Notes

- Focus on substance - highlight impact and outcomes
- Identify patterns and trends across the week
- Keep it concise but comprehensive
- If less than a full week of data, note this in the summary
